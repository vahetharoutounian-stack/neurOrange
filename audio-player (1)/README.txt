Drop your audio files into the tracks folder and name them by order:

01.mp3 → left button (🍊), starts at 10% volume (slider shows 85%)
02.mp3 → middle button (🟠), plays at 100% volume
03.mp3 → right button (✨), plays at 100% volume

Supported: .mp3 .m4a .aac .wav .ogg .mp4
Open index.html in Safari (not a previewer). Keep the tracks folder next to index.html.
